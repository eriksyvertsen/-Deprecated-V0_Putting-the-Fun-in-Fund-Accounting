Module 1: Overview of Private Equity Fund Structures

A private equity fund is typically structured as a limited partnership with two main parties:

  • General Partners (GPs): They manage the fund and are responsible for investment decisions.
  • Limited Partners (LPs): They are the investors who contribute capital but have limited liability.
